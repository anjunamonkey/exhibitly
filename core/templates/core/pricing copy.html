Frequently Asked Questions

How is the 1% calculated?

The 1% fee applies only to orders placed through A/Bite-powered menus (QR codes, digital menus, kiosks). It's calculated on the total order value including food and drinks.

Can I use just A/B testing without dynamic pricing?

Yes! You can subscribe to any SaaS tier independently to run experiments on your existing menu setup.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts required. SaaS subscriptions are month-to-month. The 1% revenue share model has no minimums.

What if I have multiple locations?

The 1% pricing scales across all locations. For A/B testing, Professional supports up to 5 locations, and Enterprise offers unlimited locations.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely! Change your SaaS tier anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle.
