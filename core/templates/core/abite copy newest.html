    Real Test Examples - concrete, measurable experiments

    📦

    Expiring Inventory Recovery

    Auto-triggered flash bundles for items nearing expiry. Goal: convert otherwise-wasted stock into revenue.

    • • Trigger when inventory age > threshold
    • • Measure uplift: orders filled / waste reduced
    • • Constraint: preserve minimum margin
    💷

    Price Elasticity Lift Test

    Run segmented price variants (e.g. lunch vs dinner, locals vs tourists) to find revenue-maximising price bands.

    • • Segmented by time, location, device
    • • Track revenue per visitor, conversion rate, AOV
    • • Auto-promote statistically winning price
    📸

    Image & Description Conversion Test

    Compare photo styles and descriptive copy to identify which presentation drives orders and reduces hesitation.

    • • Studio vs lifestyle imagery
    • • Short vs descriptive copy variants
    • • Metrics: click-to-order rate, time-to-order
    👨‍🍳

    Capacity-aware Upsell Bundles

    Offer bundles or limited combos when kitchen capacity is underused to increase throughput and AOV.

    • • Trigger during low prep-load windows
    • • Measure bundle attach rate & margin impact
    • • Automatically scale offers by capacity

    A/Bite automatically calculates statistical significance and promotes winning variants.

    Live Variant Targeting

    Run split experiments in production - different tables, device types or customer segments can see different menu variants simultaneously. Promote winners automatically.

    Real Time Optimisation

    A/Bite's AI continuously monitors signals and updates prices and placements automatically - no manual work required. Changes roll out instantly across menus, apps and POS.

    Smart Happy Hours

    Schedule dynamic happy hours triggered by low footfall or set to recurring windows.

    Weekend & Event Sales

    Plan weekend bundles, festival offers, or lunch specials that auto-activate.

    • • Scheduled campaigns with countdowns
    • • Dynamic bundling / upsell rules
    • • Channel targeting (QR, app, web)

    Everything syncs in real time across your POS, QR menus and apps - schedule, target, and measure without manual updates.
